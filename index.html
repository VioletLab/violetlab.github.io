[ About ]

Explore a new idea, join a team, or keep pushing a project you already love. VioletLab is the room where it actually gets made. Where music, art, and design meet engineering, and you ship things you're proud of.

No membership. No gatekeeping. If you want to build, you belong here.

[ Programs ]

  1. 01

    Mini hackathons

    Fast two-hour builds to get the reps in. Low stakes, high energy, make something by the time it's over.

  2. 02

    Creative jams

    Music, art, and design meet code. Live-coding visuals, music-tech, physical computing.

  3. 03

    Hack Day

    Our north star. A full weekend hackathon, nine to twelve hours, that we're working toward running with Major League Hacking.

  4. 04

    The open org

    Everything made here lives in the open on GitHub. Explore an idea, join a team, or continue an existing project.

Every session ends with something real.

A repo, a demo, a thing that runs. For first-timers and seasoned hackers alike. You leave holding what you made.
